FAQ

Everything you need to know before your first session

From memberships to HYROX and youth coaching, these quick answers help busy Staten Islanders plan their first visit with confidence.

What happens during the No-Sweat Intro? +

You’ll meet 1:1 with a coach for about 20 minutes. We review your goals, movement history, schedule, and tour the space. No workout—just a plan for your first 90 days and next steps.

Do I need CrossFit experience to get started? +

Nope. Over half of new members are brand new. Everyone starts with our LaunchPad On-Ramp so you learn foundational movements, scaling options, and how classes run before you jump into the group.

How do I reserve a class or drop-in? +

Members book through the CrossFit 103 app (powered by Wodify). Traveling athletes can use the Drop-In button above, pick a class time, submit payment, and we’ll send arrival instructions plus the door code.

Is CrossFit 103 part of the Active & Fit network? +

Yes! Bring your Active & Fit membership ID to your intro. You’ll pay the program fee through Active & Fit and a $12 monthly facility fee directly to us. You still complete LaunchPad before joining classes.

What’s included with the HYROX affiliate program? +

HYROX sessions run Thursday evenings and Sunday mornings. Members can jump in at no extra cost. Non-members can purchase a HYROX Drop-In pass which unlocks the class, prep guide, and event registration tips.

Do you coach kids or teens? +

Yes. Our Stronger with Sam youth program trains ages 10–17 in 8-week cycles focused on confidence, discipline, and movement fundamentals. Parents book a Youth Consult so Coach Sam can tailor the plan and share waivers.

Can I add nutrition coaching or personal training? +

Absolutely. Nutrition coaching is led by Coach Sophia with flexible packages. Personal training is available with Coach Mike or the coach that fits your goals. Mention it on your intro form and we’ll bundle the right plan.

Do you offer Fit3D scans or body composition testing? +

We run Fit3D scans in-house. Members get discounted scans, and non-members can book á la carte. Choose a time slot, receive prep instructions, and we’ll email the report with next-step recommendations.

What if I need to pause or cancel my membership? +

Just email the front desk 14 days before your next billing date. We can freeze memberships for travel, injury, or season conflicts. When you’re ready to return, we’ll run a quick check-in to catch you up.

Deep Dive FAQs

Staten Island specifics our members ask every day

Browse detailed answers about pricing, schedules, HYROX prep, and youth programs so you can choose the track that fits your routine.

How much does CrossFit 103 cost per month? +

Our most popular Unlimited plan is $219/month. Training three days weekly is $179/month, and Hybrid Coaching with bi-weekly 1:1 sessions is $349/month. All options begin with LaunchPad On-Ramp.

Does CrossFit 103 have an unlimited membership? +

Yes. Unlimited members can attend any coach-led class, HYROX sessions, and Open Gym hours. They also receive quarterly body composition scans or InBody check-ins.

Can I pay per class at CrossFit 103? +

We recommend memberships for consistency, but travelers and curious locals can purchase a Drop-In pass or a short-term pack. Drop-In rates include class access plus the day’s programming notes.

Where is CrossFit 103 located? +

We’re at 47 Trioka Way, Staten Island, NY 10309. Look for the black and red signage near the Corporate Park. The entry faces the side parking lot.

Does CrossFit 103 have parking? +

Yes. Free lot parking surrounds the building and there is street parking along Trioka Way during peak class times.

Is public transportation available to CrossFit 103? +

The S55 and S56 buses stop less than a five-minute walk away on Arthur Kill Road. Many commuters rideshare directly to the front entrance.

What time are the early morning classes? +

Sunrise Strength runs Monday, Wednesday, and Friday at 5:30 am, 6:30 am, and 7:30 am. Members often arrive 10 minutes early to warm up in the mobility bay.

Does CrossFit 103 offer lunchtime workouts? +

Yes. Noon Express meets weekdays at 12:00 pm for a fast-paced 60-minute class with abbreviated transitions for working professionals.

Are there evening CrossFit classes? +

Evening Crew classes start at 4:30 pm, 5:45 pm, and 7:00 pm Monday through Thursday. Friday features a 5:30 pm community workout followed by Open Gym.

Does CrossFit 103 have Open Gym hours? +

Yes. Members can reserve Open Gym during our normal business hours through the CrossFit 103 app. Time blocks vary by day, and the schedule updates weekly so you always know when the floor is available.

What equipment is in the strength deck? +

We have Rogue rig lanes, competition barbells, jerk blocks, kilo plates, GHDs, sleds, Echo bikes, Concept2 machines, sandbags, yokes, and specialty bars for strongman work.

Does CrossFit 103 program HYROX-specific workouts? +

Yes. Our HYROX affiliate sessions mirror race stations (sled pulls, wall balls, sled pushes, runs) and include strategy briefings for Staten Island and regional events.

Can non-members train for HYROX at CrossFit 103? +

Non-members can purchase a HYROX Drop-In pass or 6-week prep bundle. You’ll receive class access, the HYROX prep guide, and remote homework between sessions.

What ages qualify for the Stronger with Sam youth program? +

The program serves athletes 10–17. We group sessions by middle school and high school with tailored strength, speed, and confidence work.

Is there a separate teen membership? +

Yes. Youth cycles run in 8-week terms with pricing shared during the parent consult. Teens graduating into adult classes shift to a standard membership with coach approval.

Who leads nutrition coaching at CrossFit 103? +

Coach Sophia Nguyen (CF-L1, Precision Nutrition L1) runs our nutrition services. She offers habit-based coaching, macro plans, and accountability check-ins.

Does CrossFit 103 provide meal plans? +

We provide customizable frameworks and macro targets rather than rigid meal plans. Sophia offers sample menus and grocery lists aligned to your goals.

Who runs personal training sessions? +

Coach Mike leads most PT, but we match you with the coach best suited to your goals (strength, rehab, sport prep). Sessions can be 30 or 60 minutes.

Can I do personal training plus classes? +

Yes. Our Hybrid Coaching membership combines unlimited classes, custom accessory plans, and bi-weekly personal training to accelerate progress.

Are Fit3D scans available to the public? +

Absolutely. Members receive discounted scans; non-members can book single scans or packages. Results include posture analysis, circumference changes, and progress tracking tools.

Do I need to prep for my Fit3D scan? +

For best accuracy, schedule scans at the same time of day, wear fitted clothing, and avoid heavy meals or training 2 hours prior. Instructions arrive via email when you book.

Does CrossFit 103 accept SilverSneakers or Renew Active? +

We currently partner with Active & Fit. We’re not in the SilverSneakers or Renew Active networks yet, but we can provide invoices if your provider reimburses fitness memberships.

How do Active & Fit payments work each month? +

You pay your standard Active & Fit fee through their portal. CrossFit 103 bills a separate $12 facility fee monthly that covers specialty programming and events.

Does CrossFit 103 host competitions or throwdowns? +

Yes. We run seasonal HYROX simulations, community throwdowns, and charity WODs. Events are posted on our Events page and Instagram link-in-bio.

Can I rent the space for private training? +

Limited private rentals are available during off-peak hours for specialty coaches or teams. Email the front desk with your desired time, equipment needs, and insurance.

Are there showers at CrossFit 103? +

We have two private changing rooms with showers, stocked with towels and eco-friendly toiletries. Most members bring their own lock for personal items.

Is childcare available? +

We don’t have on-site childcare, but our schedule includes parent-friendly time slots and we partner with a nearby drop-in daycare that offers class-time coverage.

Does CrossFit 103 have a Masters program? +

Yes. The Masters 40+ track offers lower-impact progressions, mobility focus, and quarterly labs on recovery and hormone health. It’s part of the Masters membership tier.

How many coaches are on staff? +

We have 12 certified coaches, including CF-L2s, a Doctor of Physical Therapy, and nutrition specialists. Many started as members and moved into coaching roles.

What certifications do CrossFit 103 coaches hold? +

Certifications include CF-L1/L2, USA Weightlifting, Precision Nutrition, Functional Range Conditioning, and specialty courses in adaptive training and breathwork.

What is the cancellation policy? +

Send written notice 14 days before your next billing date. We’ll confirm the final draft and invite you to a comeback session whenever you’re ready to return.

How does a drop-in pay and check in? +

Book online, receive the confirmation email with door instructions, arrive 10 minutes early, and check in with the front desk. Payment is handled during booking.

Can I bring a friend to class? +

Members receive monthly buddy passes. Guest must fill out the waiver and attend a coach-led orientation before class if they are new to CrossFit.

Does CrossFit 103 offer corporate wellness or team training? +

Yes. We design custom programs for local businesses, first responders, and sports teams. Packages include on-site workshops and quarterly testing.

How long are classes at CrossFit 103? +

Standard classes run 60 minutes. HYROX sessions are 75 minutes to accommodate longer intervals and skill practice.

Does CrossFit 103 publish programming in advance? +

Yes. Members can see the week’s programming inside the CrossFit 103 app each Sunday night, including scaling notes and suggested recovery protocols.

Is there a CrossFit 103 mobile app? +

We use the Wodify app for reservations, whiteboard scores, leaderboards, and performance tracking. Your login is created after LaunchPad.

Can CrossFit 103 modify workouts for injuries? +

Definitely. Our coaches and in-house PT provide movement alternatives, tempo adjustments, and rehab plans so you can keep training safely.

Does CrossFit 103 have recovery amenities? +

Not at the moment. We focus on high-quality coaching and training space. Coaches provide mobility and recovery homework you can do at home or with your preferred practitioner.

What should I bring to my first class? +

Wear training shoes, bring water, and pack a sweat towel if desired. We supply all equipment. Arrive 10 minutes early so your coach can review scaling options.

Is there a community Slack or Facebook group? +

Members are invited to the CrossFit 103 Slack workspace after LaunchPad. It includes class updates, nutrition threads, HYROX meetups, and social events.

How do I contact CrossFit 103? +

Call (718) 555-0103, text the same number, or email hello@crossfit103.com. During business hours a coach or front desk specialist replies within minutes.
