Youth Strength & Conditioning

Stronger with Sam builds confident Staten Island athletes.

Our 8-week youth cycles blend age-appropriate strength, speed, and character coaching so middle and high school athletes develop safely and stay excited to train.

Get Started

Who it’s built for

Stronger with Sam serves athletes ages 10–17 who need structured training that supports school sports, club seasons, or general fitness.

Middle School

Build coordination, body awareness, and confidence in a screen-free environment that makes hard work fun.

High School

Develop strength, speed, and conditioning with programming that complements varsity schedules and reduces injury risk.

Multi-Sport Athletes

Balance training across seasons with coaching that monitors fatigue, keeps mobility sharp, and supports recovery.

What each cycle includes

Every block follows a clear progression so athletes and parents can track improvements week after week.

Movement Foundations

  • Skill sessions on squats, hinges, presses, and core stability with strict attention to form.
  • Speed and agility drills that train footwork, reaction, and change of direction.

Performance Tracking

  • Week 1 baseline testing with follow-up assessments at the end of each cycle.
  • Parent updates highlighting wins, focus points, and at-home guidance.

Support & Accountability

  • Optional Fit3D scans for older athletes to monitor posture and balance.
  • Coach access for questions on nutrition, recovery, and game-day routines.

Benefits for athletes and parents

Stronger with Sam isn’t just about getting sweaty—it’s about building durable athletes and confident humans.

Athletic Confidence

When athletes know how to move and recover, they show up to practices and tryouts ready to contribute.

Injury Prevention

Balanced strength work, mobility, and movement mechanics reinforce good habits that carry into every sport.

Structured Accountability

Weekly check-ins keep attendance consistent, and parents stay informed with clear communication.
